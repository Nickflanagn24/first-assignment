About Our Academy

Welcome to Kick and Connect Football Camp!

At Kick and Connect Football Camp, we believe that every young footballer deserves the opportunity to develop their skills in a supportive, fun, and challenging environment. Our camp is dedicated to fostering both athletic and personal growth, focusing on teamwork, discipline, and a love for the game. What sets us apart is our commitment to high-quality coaching—delivered by experienced professionals who have a proven track record at the highest levels of football. Our top coaches, Matt Pearson and Max Robertson, bring a wealth of knowledge, passion, and energy to the camp. Their goal is to provide each player with the tools they need to improve their technical abilities, tactical understanding, and overall fitness, while building the confidence and character that will help them succeed on and off the pitch.


Whether you are looking to take your first steps in football or aiming to sharpen your skills for competitive play, Kick and Connect Football Camp is the perfect place to learn, grow, and excel. With personalized attention from our expert coaches, players of all abilities will find themselves developing their skills and passion for the game in a motivating and professional environment.

Matt Pearson - Head Coach

With over 15 years of professional coaching experience, Matt Pearson brings a wealth of knowledge and passion to the Kick and Connect Football Camp. Matt has coached at both youth and professional levels, guiding players to reach their full potential on the field. Known for his hands-on approach, he excels at breaking down complex skills into easy-to-understand techniques, making learning both effective and enjoyable. Certified by top soccer associations, Matt’s expertise lies in technical skills development and game strategy. His dedication to building confidence in young athletes and fostering teamwork ensures that every player leaves the camp with improved abilities and a greater love for the game.

Max Robertson - Assistant Head Coach

Max Robertson is a seasoned football coach with over a decade of experience coaching youth and professional teams. His focus on fitness and conditioning, paired with a deep understanding of game tactics, makes him an integral part of the Kick and Connect Football Camp. Max has a unique ability to connect with players of all ages, helping them push their limits while creating a supportive and positive training environment. Max holds advanced certifications in soccer coaching and athlete development, and his commitment to both the physical and mental growth of young athletes is a key aspect of his coaching philosophy. His energy and enthusiasm inspire campers to work hard and have fun while improving their skills.
